Our Story – From setback to success

A journey of resilience, reinvention, and growth.

Humble Beginnings

Spybugs started as a small computer shop, serving the local community with basic computer access and services.

Transformation

When the pandemic hit, the business struggled and eventually went bankrupt—but the story didn’t end there.

Success & Growth

Jeremi and Gissle chose to rebuild Spybugs as a dedicated printing shop, leading to stronger operations and better opportunities.

Spybugs originally began as a computer shop, but during the pandemic, the business struggled and eventually went bankrupt.

Instead of giving up, Jeremi and Gissle—the owners—chose to rebuild and transform Spybugs into a printing shop. This decision became the turning point of the business.

With more focused service and improved operations, Spybugs began to grow again and earn more than it ever did before. Today, Spybugs proudly serves the Taal community with reliable printing, photo services, online assistance, and more.
